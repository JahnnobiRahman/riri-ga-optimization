Response generator (core behavior the GA “optimizes”) (L200–L262)
L204–L218: determines:

L219–L232: converts weights into structure decisions:
empathy weight controls how many empathy lines
structure weight controls whether to include grounding/action/question
safety weight can add extra safety reminder
L233–L261: builds the response:
adds [TEMPLATE] ... and [SAFETY] ...
adds empathy lines
conditionally adds escalation lines
adds structure components (grounding/action/question)
truncates if memory_window <= 256
Key idea: the GA gets signal because different genomes produce measurably different text patterns.

L298–L301: length_penalty penalizes very long outputs (>140 words).
